Welcome to Tour des Canadiens 2, a prestigious high-rise condo building nestled in Downtown Montreal. Built in 2019, this 54-level marvel boasts 590 Montreal condos, each a masterpiece of modern living. With a luxurious marble-accented lobby and a four-storey atrium, this architectural wonder connects seamlessly to Windsor Station and the Bell Centre via a Skybridge. Unwind at Montreal’s first Skylounge on the top floor, offering a bar and panoramic views that will take your breath away. Nearby, you’ll find iconic attractions like Bell Centre, Notre-Dame Basilica, and Old Port. Enjoy easy access to Lucien-L’Allier metro station (orange line) and multiple STM bus lines, making city exploration a breeze. Work and play harmonize perfectly at this urban oasis.
